WHO WE ARE

Sinergia Animal is an international animal protection organization working in countries of the Global South to reduce the suffering of farmed animals and promote more compassionate food choices.

We are proudly "born and raised" in the Global South, and we solely dedicate our work to this area, where animals need us so much. We currently have teams within six countries of Southeast Asia and Latin America.
